Operation Enduring Freedom

by Admin Dave
landscape Terrain: Everon
history v1.2.0

Soviet forces have established a heavily fortified communications relay at Montignac. Intelligence indicates this site is coordinating a major offensive across the southern sector of Everon. The US garrison at Morton has been tasked with a preemptive strike to cripple their command structure before the assault begins. Time is critical, and resistance is expected to be heavy.

Your platoon will insert via MH-60 Blackhawk insertions at LZ Alpha, 2km south of the objective. Establish a secure perimeter, neutralize the external defenses, and secure the primary relay building. Expect mechanized QRF from the north within 15 minutes of initial contact. Coordinate with attached mortar sections for preliminary bombardment.

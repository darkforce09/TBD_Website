# Eden_Editor:_Transforming_Crew

Source: https://community.bohemia.net/wiki/Eden_Editor:_Transforming_Crew

---

### Overview

Characters can become vehicle crew members, while still being independently editable entities.
The available vehicle roles are:

-  Driver / Pilot - controls vehicle movement

-  Commander - can issue orders to other crew members.

-  Turret - any other active crew member, such as the gunner, co-pilot, or passenger with the ability to shoot from the vehicle

-  Passenger - passive role, just takes a ride

### Crew in the editor interface

When you hover over a vehicle, a panel with all the crew icons will be shown next to the vehicle's icon.

The additional icon is shown on the crew members which are rendered in the scene (i.e., the car driver, but not a tank driver who is hidden inside).

### Moving crew into a vehicle

To move a character into a vehicle, drag it onto the vehicle's icon or model. If there is no space left, the character will only be moved to the vehicle's position.

ⓘYou will be notified when attempting to drag a character into an enemy vehicle. While it is possible, it can cause problems in the scenario.

### Removing crew from a vehicle

To move the character out, drag its icon out again.

### Changing Seats

You can change the seat of a crew member. Click right mouse button on the crew member, expand Change Seat option and pick the new seat from the list.
If someone else is sitting on the desired seat, the crew members will swap seats.
